Beekeeping in Balance — Environment and Stewardship

Last updated: 06 Oct 2025

Sustainable apiculture means improving habitat, reducing pesticide risks and adapting to climate shifts.

Key takeaways

  • Forage diversity is foundational for health.
  • Pesticide vigilance and mitigation protect bees and products.
  • Climate variability requires adaptive management and stock.
  • Support biodiversity: right hive density, habitat creation.
