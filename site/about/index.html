About Bee Planet Connection

We serve global apiculture as an open hub for knowledge, tools, a directory, and discussion — championing environmental stewardship in beekeeping.

What BPC is

  • A free knowledge base: An open wiki of referenced articles from bee biology to best practices.
  • A practical toolkit: Templates, checklists, and calculators for day-to-day and seasonal tasks.
  • A global directory: Organisations, research bodies, and networks in apiculture.
  • A community forum: Thoughtful discussion, Q&A, and peer support.

What BPC is not

  • Not a marketplace.
  • Not a regulatory authority — always follow local rules and veterinary advice.
  • Not a closed club — we welcome constructive contributions from all.

Contribute

  • Suggest edits via GitHub pull requests.
  • Propose directory additions.
  • Share tools and templates.
  • Join discussions and mentor newcomers.
