Open, practical, and planet-minded apiculture.

Bee Planet Connection (BPC) is a global platform uniting beekeepers, researchers, and enthusiasts. Our aim is to share knowledge and tools that empower sustainable beekeeping across continents.

Why it matters

Bees and other pollinators underpin biodiversity and food security. By sharing knowledge and promoting sustainable practices, BPC helps beekeepers care for colonies, restore forage habitats, and reduce harmful exposures — protecting pollinators for future generations.
