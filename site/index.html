Connecting beekeepers worldwide with knowledge, tools, and community.

Why it matters

Bees and other pollinators underpin biodiversity and food security. We promote practical, referenced knowledge and tools that help beekeepers care for colonies, restore forage, and reduce harmful exposures.

What is Bee Planet Connection?

  • A free knowledge base: An open wiki of referenced articles from bee biology to best practices.
  • A practical toolkit: Templates, checklists, and calculators for day-to-day and seasonal tasks.
  • A global directory: Organisations, research bodies, and networks in apiculture.
  • A community forum: Thoughtful discussion, Q&A, and peer support.
