Bee Planet Connection helps people understand bee biology, practice evidence-based beekeeping, and connect with others who care about pollinators. We publish clear, peer-informed articles, hands-on tools, and structured learning paths—from first hive to advanced practice.

Start with the Wiki for fundamentals, visit the Blog for ideas and field notes, and use our Tools and Directory to put knowledge to work.
