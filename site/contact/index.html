Contact Bee Planet Connection

Connecting beekeepers worldwide with knowledge, tools, and community.

Open, practical, and planet-minded apiculture for real people doing real work. Bee Planet Connection (BPC) brings together beekeepers, researchers, and enthusiasts across borders. We share tools, guides, and ideas that make sustainable beekeeping easier—wherever you are.

Why it matters

Bees and other pollinators underpin biodiversity and food security. By sharing knowledge and promoting sustainable practices, we help beekeepers care for colonies, restore forage habitats, and reduce harmful exposures — protecting pollinators for future generations.
