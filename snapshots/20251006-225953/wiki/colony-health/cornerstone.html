Keeping the Hive Healthy — Threats and Remedies

Last updated: 06 Oct 2025

From Varroa to foulbrood, colony health depends on informed monitoring, nutrition and timely interventions grounded in IPM.

Key takeaways

  • Varroa management is essential for survival in most regions.
  • IPM combines cultural, mechanical and chemical tools to limit pests.
  • Nutrition and diverse forage strengthen immunity and brood rearing.
  • Biosecurity and legal reporting protect your bees and your community.
